Chocolate Chip Cookies

Overview

Crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside cookies loaded with chocolate chips.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup chocolate chips

Substitutions

Use vegan butter and flax eggs for a vegan version. Substitute white chocolate chips for a different flavor.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream butter and sugar, add eggs, then mix in flour and chips.
  3. Drop spoonfuls onto baking sheet and bake for 12 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 150 calories per cookie.
