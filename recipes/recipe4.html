Vanilla Ice Cream

Overview

Smooth and creamy vanilla ice cream, perfect for summer.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Substitutions

Use coconut milk for a dairy-free version. Add fruit puree for extra flavor.

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Churn in ice cream maker or freeze, stirring every 30 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 200 calories per serving.
