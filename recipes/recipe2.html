Classic Apple Pie

Overview

Traditional apple pie with a flaky crust and sweet apple filling.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 4 apples, sliced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Substitutions

Use margarine instead of butter for a vegan crust. Try pears instead of apples for a twist.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Prepare crust and fill with apple mixture.
  3. Bake for 45 minutes until golden brown.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 300 calories per slice.
