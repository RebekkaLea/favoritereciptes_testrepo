Lemon Bars

Overview

Tangy and sweet lemon bars with a buttery crust.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 3 lemons, juiced
  • 3 eggs

Substitutions

Use lime juice for a different flavor. Substitute margarine for butter.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Prepare crust, pour lemon mixture over, bake for 25 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 180 calories per bar.
