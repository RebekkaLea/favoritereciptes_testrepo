Carrot Cake

Overview

Moist carrot cake with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups grated carrots

Substitutions

Use applesauce instead of oil for a lighter cake. Add walnuts or raisins for extra texture.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix ingredients, pour into pan, bake for 35 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 230 calories per slice.
