Pumpkin Pie

Overview

Classic pumpkin pie with a spiced filling and flaky crust - and lots of deliciousness.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups pumpkin puree
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1 tsp cinnamon

Substitutions

Use coconut cream for a dairy-free version. Add nutmeg for extra spice.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Mix ingredients, pour into crust, bake for 50 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 240 calories per slice.
