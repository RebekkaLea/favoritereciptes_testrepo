Strawberry Shortcake

Overview

Light and fluffy shortcake topped with fresh strawberries and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 cups strawberries
  • Whipped cream

Substitutions

Use gluten-free flour for a gluten-free version. Substitute blueberries for strawberries.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, bake shortcake for 20 minutes.
  3. Top with strawberries and whipped cream.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 220 calories per serving.
