Chocolate Cake

Overview

Rich and moist chocolate cake perfect for celebrations.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk

Substitutions

Use almond milk for a dairy-free option. Substitute coconut sugar for regular sugar.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix dry ingredients together.
  3. Add eggs and milk, mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 30 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 250 calories per slice.
