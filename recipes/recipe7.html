Banana Bread

Overview

Moist and flavorful banana bread, great for breakfast or dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 ripe bananas

Substitutions

Use applesauce instead of eggs for a vegan version. Add walnuts for crunch.

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mash bananas, mix with other ingredients, bake for 50 minutes.

Nutritional Information

Approx. 200 calories per slice.
